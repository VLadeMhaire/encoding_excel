Percentage Tax (Section 116)
For non-VAT registered taxpayers with gross receipts ≤ ₱3,000,000 · NIRC Sec. 116
₱
Percentage Tax Notes
Percentage Tax is filed using BIR Form 2551Q on a quarterly basis, due on or before the 25th day of the month following the close of each taxable quarter.
Once your gross sales exceed ₱3,000,000 in a 12-month period, you must register for VAT and shift from Percentage Tax to VAT filing.
